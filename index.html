Our Services

At Universal Technology & Services, we offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our expertise spans across various sectors, ensuring top-quality solutions for every project.

Design & Engineering

Our team of skilled designers and engineers work closely with clients to develop innovative, efficient, and practical solutions. We bring your ideas to life, from initial concept to final design, ensuring every detail aligns with your vision and requirements.

Manufacturing

We specialize in creating high-quality cable trays and modular support systems, essential for various industrial applications. Our manufacturing processes are streamlined for efficiency, ensuring timely and reliable production.

Fabrication (Sheetmetal)

Our state-of-the-art sheet metal fabrication services are perfect for a wide range of applications. We pride ourselves on our precision and ability to create complex designs with impeccable accuracy and durability.

Electrical Panel Manufacturing

We produce top-of-the-line electrical panels, designed for reliability and safety. Our panels are manufactured to meet industry standards, suitable for various applications in commercial and industrial settings.

Acoustic Treatment

Our acoustic treatment solutions are ideal for managing noise levels in various environments. From soundproofing to acoustic optimization, we ensure that your space maintains the desired level of tranquility and sound quality.

Light Mast

We provide robust and durable light mast solutions, suitable for illuminating large outdoor areas. Our light masts are designed for easy installation and long-term reliability, ensuring optimal lighting for any setting.

Utility Project Execution

Our expertise extends to comprehensive utility project execution, managing everything from planning to implementation. We focus on delivering efficient, reliable solutions for utility infrastructure projects.

Fire Safety System

We design and install advanced fire safety systems, essential for protecting property and lives. Our team ensures that each system meets all safety regulations and client specifications for maximum protection.

Trading

In addition to our fabrication and engineering services, we engage in trading, offering a variety of high-quality materials and components. Our extensive network and expertise in the industry allow us to provide the best products at competitive prices.
